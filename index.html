About Me

I’m a UI/UX Designer with 2 years of experience crafting digital experiences that are intuitive, functional, and people-first.

My design journey began with a simple love for solving everyday problems, sketching out ideas just to make things work better. A close friend, a full-stack developer, introduced me to the world of UI/UX.

Since then, I’ve been blending creativity with strategy, designing with empathy, prototyping in Figma, and building interactive experiences with HTML/CSS in VS Code.

I'm always learning and evolving, whether it's picking up new tools or uncovering better ways to serve users. Growth is part of how I design.
